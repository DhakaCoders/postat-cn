Our luxurious amenities, in harmony with sustainable living, offer the ideal setting for you to disconnect and recharge. Set away from the hustle of city life, our enviable location is only eighty minutes from Sydney. From spellbinding vistas all to yourself, to our thoughtful and welcoming spaces, POST at Oakgate provides an opportunity to slow down and reconnect.

The dark, neutral-toned cabins provide the perfect setting for beautiful memories of your escape from the outside world. The natural ambience of the great outdoors beyond your cabin is enhanced by a picturesque outdoor fire pit, a yoga and meditation platform and landscaping. A large, luxurious bath is set outside on the raised decking for a relaxing, bathing experience that is truly out of this world.

Step inside the cabin to discover the balance and harmony between the simplicity of the design and the elegance of the natural furniture. In keeping with our steadfast commitment to quality without compromising the environment, furnishings are locally sourced from producers of fine-quality, ethically and sustainably produced furniture.

Heating and cooling are provided by an indoor fire place and solar powered air-conditioning, and concrete floors with under-floor heating provide comfortable passage throughout. Each of our sumptuous cabins features two bedrooms, exquisitely furnished with a queen-sized bed and a queen loft bed comfortably accommodating up to 4 guests.

The bathrooms feature quality fittings and a large rainfall shower, with solar power providing the heat to the rainwater sustainably collected by each cabin. Complimentary guest amenities are sourced from high-end suppliers and are certified cruelty free.

Enjoy a leisurely start to the day with breakfast in the comfort of your cabin, with bread, milk, spreads and hot beverages included for your convenience. The fully equipped kitchen goes beyond tea and coffee making facilities, allowing you the freedom to cater your own meals.

The feature, sliding doors in the main bedroom and living area have been architecturally designed to invite the wonder of your surroundings inside, allowing an uninterrupted view of the stunning mountain ranges beyond the lush grounds of the estate. You can choose to wake up to the beauty beyond or draw the curtains for complete privacy.

The cabins are equipped with high-speed WiFi and subscription television is included for your entertainment in the living area, making it the perfect place to relax and unwind after the day’s activities. The plush reading nook, set away from the living area, in each cabin provides a tranquil space within which to indulge in a good book.
